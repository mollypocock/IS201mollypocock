Passion

Bridle Up Hope

Instructor, Service Lead

Since begining my work at this non-profit in August of 2023, I have loved every moment of it. The mission of Bridle Up Hope is to inspire hope, confidence and resilience in girls and women through horses and habits. Here I work with girls with lots of different backgrounds, befriending them and helping them to gain confidence in themselves. I teach about the 7 Habits of Highly Effective People by Stephen Covey, integrated with horsemanship to my students in weekly 1-on-1 lssons.
